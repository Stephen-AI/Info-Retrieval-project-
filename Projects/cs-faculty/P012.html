Our faculty are at the heart of what has made the University of Texas at Austin one of the most successful institutions in the world at advancing the field of computer science. This university is home to Turing Award winners, members of the National Academies of Engineering and of Sciences, Computers and Thought Award winners, Simons Investigators, and myriad faculty redefining the frontiers of computer science research and education.
