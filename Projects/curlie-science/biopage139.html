SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING OF BIOSYSTEMS

    Welcome to the global information network for science and engineering of biosystem, which was created by The Networks for Biosystem Science and Engineering (NABSE) in 1999. The concepts of systems medicine, system (structure - pattern) genetics, systems biological engineering and oviduct bioreactor were published from 1992 to 1994 (B. J. Zeng, Structurity: Pan-Evolution Theory, May, 1994). System bioscience or biosystem science, which inludes 1)Systems Medicine and Pharmocology, 2)Systems Genetics and Bio-Engineering, 3)Systems and Synthetic Biology, 4)Systems Biotechnology and Neuroscience, will be explored at the aspects of genomic and psychological biosystems, such as biological, cognitive and computation systems etc. for the advancement of bio-agriculture, bio-medicine and bio-industrials.
