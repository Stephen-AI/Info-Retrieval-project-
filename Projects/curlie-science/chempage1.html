Hints:

  • Links of S phrases point to the Council Directive 67/548/EEC. You will find there notes on how to use the S-phrase.
  • In this list you will also find some modifications of S phrases made by manufacturers. These modifications are preceded by the company name.

S20: When using do not eat or drink.

S28: After contact with skin, wash immediately with plenty of ... (to be specified by the manufacturer).

Riedel-de Haen S43A: In case of fire use sand. Never use water.
