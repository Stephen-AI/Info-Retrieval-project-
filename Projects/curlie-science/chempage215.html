Chemometrics is the discipline based on design strategies and multivariate statistical tools for a better understanding, usually solving, complex chemical problems.

Company profile

 MIA is a small company producing and distributing scientific software. This embeds chemometric strategies and expertise devoted to solve complex chemical problems according to the philosophy developed at the Laboratorio di Chemiometria of the University of Perugia (Italy) by Prof. Sergio Clementi and Dr. Gabriele Cruciani.
