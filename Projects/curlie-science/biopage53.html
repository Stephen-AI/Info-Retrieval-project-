Indian Journal of Gastroenterology
This is the web site of Indian Journal of Gastroenterology (IJG), the official publication of the Indian Society of Gastroenterology (ISG). This site includes full text of all articles published from 1982, and abstracts and full text of selected articles published between 1995 and 2011. This Journal is Indexed/abstracted in PubMed/Medline, SCOPUS, EMBASE, Google Scholar, CAB International, Academic OneFile, CAB Abstracts, Expanded Academic, Global Health, Indian Science Abstracts, IndMed, MedInd, OCLC, SCImago, Summon by ProQuest.
