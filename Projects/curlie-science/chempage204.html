home / nonesuch

Ooops - You have reached a University of Michigan web server operated by the Information Technology Services Web Hosting service by mistake.

Possible reasons for this are (in no particular order):

  • You tried to reach a web site hosted by us using an IP address instead of by name.
  • You tried to reach a web site no longer provisioned by us, but where the domain name registration (incorrectly) refers to our servers.
  • You used a web browser (client) that does not properly support Server Name indication in the http request.
    • In particular, obsolete versions of Internet Explorer (IE) do not support Server Name Indication on secure (https) connections - Your browser should also have reported that the cert (certificate) recieved from our server did not match the name.
  • The new web site you have requested of us has not been provisioned yet.

Feel free to visit our services page, e-mail webmaster@umich.edu, or to contact us.

Members of the University of Michigan community can also visit a wiki at http://knowledgebase.umich.edu/. In particular, developers of web services at the University of Michigan may find the "Web Technologies" article helpful.
