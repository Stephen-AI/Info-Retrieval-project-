Brandywine Research Laboratory originated in 1970 offering Infrared (IR) analysis services. Over the years we have continued to develop IR methods and techniques to resolve a wide range of chemically related problems. IR is a powerful tool to identify unknown materials or verify known substances.

In 1999 we moved to 60 Blue Hen Drive, Newark, DE  and shared facilities with Corrosion Testing Laboratories, Inc.  Over the past several years the two companies have merged and now function as one organization. What was formerly Brandywine Research Laboratory is now the Chemical Analysis Group of Corrosion Testing Laboratories, Inc.

Please visit Corrosion Testing Laboratories, Inc.’s website for a description of our Chemical Analysis capabilities as well as the additional capabilities of Corrosion Testing Laboratories.
