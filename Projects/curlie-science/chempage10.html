The atomic weight suitable between parentheses is reported to the mass of most stable know isotope.

For the elements with atomic number superior to 103 has stayed proposed a standard method of attribution of the name, fully grown from corresponding prefixes to each figure of the atomic number, which addition comes an ending (106 = Un-nil-hex-io = Une).
104 = Unnilquadio (Unq) realized in Urss in the 1964 and in Usa in the 1969, the two groups have respectively proposed the names kurciatovio (Ku), and rutherfordio (Rf).
105 = Unnilpenzio (Unp) realized in the 1970, and having proposed the name hahanio (Ha).
106 = Unnilhexio (Une) realized in the 1974.
107 = Unnilseptio (Uns) realized in the 1975.
109 = Unnilennio (Unn) realized in the 1982.
110 = Ununnilio (Uun) realized in the 1994.
