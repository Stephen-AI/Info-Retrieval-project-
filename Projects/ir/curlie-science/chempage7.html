News

Books Published

The book, Chemometrics : Data Analysis for the Laboratory and Chemical Plant was published by Wiley in 2003.

The book, Applied Chemometrics for Scientists was published by Wiley in 2007.

The book, Chemometrics for Pattern Recognition was published by Wiley in 2009.

Centre for Chemometrics

As of October 2003, the group has been renamed the Centre for Chemometrics to reflect the enhanced level and breadth of research activities, and moved into new custom built space.

New Space for the Centre

As of November 2005 the group enters new custom built premises within the School of Chemistry, as part of the School's £18million rebuilding project.

Prizes

Richard Brereton has been awarded the Royal Society of Chemistry's 2006 Theophilus Redwood Lectureship.

Simeone Zomer has been awarded the Royal Society of Chemistry's Ronald Belcher Memorial Lectureship.
