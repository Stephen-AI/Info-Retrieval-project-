Hyfoma Food equipment database & knowledge portal
The vision of hyfoma.com is to publish all relevant knowledge for food manufacturing and to be a market place for engineers and food processing manufacturers. Hyfoma does not supply machines, but only refers to the companies that do. Most of the suppliers listed supply machines for the food industry, and do not produce food themselves.
