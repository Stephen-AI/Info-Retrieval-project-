International Conference on Bioinformatics

The International Conference on Bioinformatics is a conference series first started in Bangkok, Thailand in 2002. Since then, the Asia Pacific Bioinformatics Network (APBioNet) has adopted this conference and grown the conference to become one of Asiaâ€™s oldest and largest conferences on bioinformatics, with special focus on bioinformatics amongst life scientists.

International Symposium on Bioinformatics

International Symposium on Bioinformatics (InSyB) bridges the gap between InCoB (International Conference on Bioinformatics) and irregular local APBioNet-supported workshops or training courses. The one or two day annual InSyB is expected to be centred on topics relevant to the local organizer.
