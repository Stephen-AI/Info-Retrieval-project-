hnRNP - K Targets Open Chromatin in Mouse Embryonic Stem Cells in Concert With Multiple Regulators
Evgeny I. Bakhmet, et al., STEM CELLS

 

Results of this study showed that the poly(C) - binding protein hnRNP‐K occupies in embryonic stem cells (ESCs) both distal and proximal enhancers of the Oct4 gene via CT - rich elements. Genome - wide analysis revealed many colocalizations of hnRNP - K with TATA - box binding protein (TBP), Oct4, Otx2, and active histone marks. Also, comparison of hnRNP - K ChIP - seq data with Mnase - seq and ATAC - seq revealed that this protein exclusively targets open chromatin of ESCs.
