About us

Founded in 1876 the Conchological Society of Great Britain and Ireland is one of the oldest existing societies devoted to the study of molluscs. The society:-

  • publishes a journal and magazine
  • runs a range of recording schemes
  • runs field and indoor meetings
  • holds training events
  • awards grants for research
  • provides advice on molluscan conservation
