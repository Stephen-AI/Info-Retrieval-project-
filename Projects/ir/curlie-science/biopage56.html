    Welcome to n2sleep

    N2Sleep Diagnostics Inc. has been the pioneer in providing outsource sleep scoring solutions to the sleep medical community since 2004. Although the vast majority of our clients are sleep diagnostics facilities and hospitals within the United States, we also service other facilities from around the world. Our entire scoring staff is registered with the BRPT.

    Our turnaround time of 24-48 hours is something that our clients constantly count on.  Along with a very fair and competitive price per study, our clients can significantly reduce the total cost of delivering quality sleep diagnostics to their patients.

    Since all studies are MANUALLY scored by a registered technologist, your physician can reliably count on the quality of the scored studies.

    Why N2Sleep Diagnostics Inc.

    1. Quality of Scoring- all studies are scored by RPSGTs
    2. Consistent turnaround time- most of our studies are scored within 24 hours. We guarantee a turnaround time of 48 hours
    3. Competitive cost per study- depending on monthly volume, studies are priced fairly and competitively from as low as  $25 per study.
    4. Stability- we’ve been in business since 2004.
    5. All studies are manually scored. We do not do auto-scoring.


    Benefits of Outsource Scoring:

    1. Isolating the scoring function dramatically improves the throughput of processing the results of the test…faster turnaround time
    2. Great “employee vacation” solution. When your regular scoring employee is off or takes a vacation, there is no disruption.
    3. Converting a fixed cost into a variable cost.

    Maximizing profits. Increasing efficiency. Enhancing patient care.
