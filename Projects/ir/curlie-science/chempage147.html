Helpers Wanted!

VAC hosts a number of events for our members each year, and rely on a network of volunteers to help us out. For just a few hours of your time, you can be reimbursed with free food and drink, VIP backstage access and you are invited to an end-of-year event solely for the helpers! If you are interested in signing up to our emailing list, or would like to know more, contact the Webmaster for more details.
