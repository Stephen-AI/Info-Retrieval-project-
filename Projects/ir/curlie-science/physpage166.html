Attendees of the World's largest X-ray conference will have access to sessions on the latest advancements in XRD and XRF. Workshops are run by experts who provide TRAINING and EDUCATION on many PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS of X-ray fluorescence and X-ray diffraction techniques for the study of materials. Designed to help attendees (with little or many years of experience), our workshops will solve some of their most difficult problems.

Advances in X-ray Analysis (AXA)

AXA Volume 63, proceedings of the 2019 DXC – if you presented at the conference, submit your work by 20 September 2019!

Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your research presented at DXC 2019 by submitting your presented paper in the Denver X-ray Conference proceedings, Advances in X-ray Analysis, Volume 63. Work presented during either a DXC oral session or poster session is eligible for submission. See the Guidelines for the Preparation of Manuscripts. The deadline for submission is 20 September 2019. 

Note: To be acceptable for publication, papers should describe either new methods, theory and applications, improvements in methods or instrumentation, or other advances in the state of the art. Papers emphasizing commercial aspects are discouraged.
