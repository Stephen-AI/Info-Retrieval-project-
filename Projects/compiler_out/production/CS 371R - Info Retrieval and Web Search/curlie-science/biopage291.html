Saltwater Inc. collects high quality data on fisheries and oceans for government agencies, research organizations, and fishermen. We are an industry leader in the design and implementation of fishery and marine mammal observer programs — and an innovator in the use of new technologies and electronic monitoring for data collection. If you are a biologist looking for a job, a client looking for an observer, or want to learn more about our electronic monitoring solutions, you’ve come to the right place.
