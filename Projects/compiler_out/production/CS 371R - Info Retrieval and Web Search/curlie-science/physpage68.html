Guestbook

This site is an attempt to document some of the physics experiments and science projects which I have conducted in my laboratory at home. These experiments include high vacuum, glass blowing, X-rays, optics and electrostatics. There is also a live interactive single photon Young's slits experiment that you can remote-control and plot your own results.
