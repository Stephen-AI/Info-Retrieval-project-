Ion Beam Centre

The Ion Beam Centre (IBC) at the University of Surrey is a national facility supported by the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC). It sits within the Advanced Technology Institute.

About the centre

Our research

We have an international reputation in the fields of ion beam applications for materials modification and analysis and house three accelerators for ion beam analysis, ion implantation and ion irradiation purposes.
