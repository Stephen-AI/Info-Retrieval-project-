Bench-top High Vacuum and Thin Film Products

Oxford Vacuum Science offers a range of compact, high performance bench-top high vacuum and thin film deposition products for research and development and light production applications. Highly modular designs allow for ease of configuration to your specific application and for straight-forward upgrade for as your requirements evolve.

Oxford Vacuum Science's product range includes:

  • Bench-top high vacuum platforms for research and development and light industrial vacuum coating, and vacuum environments for OEM instrumentation applications
  • High performance thin film depostion sources for single and multilayer vacuum deposition processes
  • High vacuum system components for substrate mounting and transfer, system bake, thin film deposition monitoring, substrate shuttering, internal jigging etc.
