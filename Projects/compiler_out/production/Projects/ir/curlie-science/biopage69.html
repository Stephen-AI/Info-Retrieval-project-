E Komo Mai Welcome to Kewalo Marine Laboratory @ the University of Hawaiʻi

 

  • Who We Are

    Kewalo Marine Laboratory is a world-class research facility specializing in the study of the biodiversity and the effects of human activity on our precious local marine environment.

  • What We Do

    Kewalo Marine Lab provides support for University of Hawaiʻi faculty, students, and visiting researchers from around the world who seek to understand life in the marine environment.

  • Where We Are

    Kewalo Marine Laboratory is located on a two-acre, ocean-front site on the south shore of O'ahu, adjacent to Kaka'ako Waterfront Park and the Kewalo Basin boat harbor.
