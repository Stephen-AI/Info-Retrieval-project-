SOLARTRON METROLOGY MEASUREMENT SENSORS AT A GLANCE

Solartron Metrology is a world leader in the design and manufacture of highly accurate, precise and rugged digital and analogue linear measurement sensors and dimensional gauging probes, including LVDT displacement transducer and gauging probes. These displacement sensor manufacturer products are complemented by a range of precise block gauges, flexure gauges and non-contact measurement sensors. This sensor technology, combined with the high speed, high resolution and accurate Orbit Digital Measurement Network or specialist instrumentation, will confidently and accurately solve your precision measurement problem.
