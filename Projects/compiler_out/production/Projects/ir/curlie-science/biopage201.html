Genomic Medicine

Genomic Medicine is an emerging medical discipline that uses genetic information about an individual to help develop a diagnosis or treatment plan. It permits doctors to customize medical care according to your bodys unique genetic makeup, and is often referred to as tailored medicine.

Contact us at 802-847-5121 or 800-991-2799 or by email.
