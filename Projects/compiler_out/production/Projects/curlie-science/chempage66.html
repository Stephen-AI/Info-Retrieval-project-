Group Description


chrom-L@yahoogroups.com



The chromatography email discussion list



A discussion list for those using any
chromatographic technique such as TLC, HPLC,
GC, and related detectors.



All messages
are archived and searchable by anyone, but to post to the group, you need
to register with chrom-L (details below).
