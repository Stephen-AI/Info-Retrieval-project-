Welcome!

The research of Professor Larry R. Dalton's group focuses on the interdisciplinary field of materials chemistry with particular emphasis on organic electro-optic, nonlinear optical, and electronic materials with an emphasis on nanoscale materials interactions, molecular modeling, high speed optical-to-electrical modulators, and novel device fabrication techniques.
