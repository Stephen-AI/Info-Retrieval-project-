Microbiology Gateway

Information, current research and relevant technology (including metagenomics, qPCR, real-time PCR, nanotechnology, genomic analysis, DNA sequencing and computational biology) on all aspects of microbiology and molecular biology. Below we provide a collection of reviews and documents on various topics in microbiology, molecular biology and PCR mainly from published books and papers written by leading scientists. These are of interest to scientists working in all areas of microbiology including bacteriology, virology, mycology, parasitology and molecular microbiology. Much of the information will also be of interest to scientists working in other areas of molecular biology, biotechnology and related disciplines.

Microbial Exopolysaccharides: Current Research and Developments
Edited by: Özlem Ateş Duru
Written by an international team of experts this authoritative volume describes the most recent and innovative research and developments on microbial exopolysaccharides.
