Cranefield Awardees

The SGP Council is pleased to announce the 2018 Paul F. Cranefield Award winners:

Cranefield Award
Anne Carlson for "PLC and IP3-evoked Ca2+ release initiate the fast block to polyspermy in Xenopus laevis eggs" Journal of General Physiology 150 (9) 1239-1248; DOI: 10.1085/jgp.201812069 and "The TMEM16A channel mediates the fast polyspermy block in Xenopus laevis Journal of General Physiology 150 (9) 1249-1259; DOI: 10.1085/jgp.201812071

Cranefield Postdoctoral Fellow Award
Gucan Dai for "Dynamic rearrangement of the intrinsic ligand regulates KCNH potassium" channels Journal of General Physiology 150 (4) 625-635; DOI: 10.1085/jgp.201711989

Cranefield Graduate Student Award
George Vaisey for "An allosteric mechanism of inactivation in the calcium-dependent chloride channel BEST1" Journal of General Physiology 150 (11): 1484-1497; 10.1085/jgp.201812190
