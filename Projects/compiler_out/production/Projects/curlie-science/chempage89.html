Our newest addition to Scientific Spectator is Personal Care China, which offers technical articles on formulations, technology, and raw materials used in the personal care market. Personal Care China is a group for cosmetic and personal care formulators, raw material suppliers and marketing professionals interested who are interested in activities in these areas that are current in China. The market in China is growing so quickly that prompt, accurate communication is a must. Also visit www.personalcarechina.com.
